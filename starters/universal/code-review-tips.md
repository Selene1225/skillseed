PR reviews should focus on: correctness, security, readability, and maintainability (in that order).
Ask "why" before suggesting changes — the author may have context you lack.
Prefer suggesting alternatives over just saying "this is wrong".
Approve with minor comments rather than blocking on style issues.
Keep PRs small (<400 lines) for effective review.
